Bar Plots

Posted on 2017.12.11

First, bring in all the neccessary tools … library(Lahman) library(sqldf) library(ggplot2)

THen, to create a bar plot we …. query<-"SELECT name,HR FROM Teams WHERE yearID=1980 ORDER BY HR" result<-sqldf(query) result$name<-factor(result$name,levels=result$name) ggplot()+ geom_bar(data=result,aes(x=name,y=HR),stat='identity',color="white",fill="blue")+ coord_flip()+ ylab("Homeruns")+ xlab("Teams")+ ggtitle("1980 Team Homerun Distribution")
